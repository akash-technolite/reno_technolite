Top 5 tips to win a ton
of new clients

  • 1
    Contact the potential client as soon as possible. The project will be submitted to several of your competitors, so the one that contacts the client the soonest, will increase their chances the most of winning the contract.
  • 2
    Make sure to provide a professional follow-up. Once again, you are competing with others who wish to get the contract, and the quality and professionalism of your service might make all the difference in the decision process.
  • 3
    Encourage your clients to give us positive feedback after the project is complete. The more positive feedback you get, the better your ranking, which leads to faster and more access to the best potential new projects.
  • 4
    Make sure to submit an estimate that is complete, precise, and most of all competitive. The client will likely have several offers and as much as professionalism and quality of service are an important criteria, the price is often a determing factor in the final decision.
  • 5
    Be available and accommodating to the potential client in terms of the desired timeline to complete the project.
